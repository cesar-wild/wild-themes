# Wild Agents — Brand Artifacts v0.1

---

## Mission

Build autonomous agents that hunt problems, ship solutions, and guard quality — as a pack.

---

## Vision

A world where software teams run like wolf packs: lean, fast, loyal, and self-organizing. Every agent knows its role. Every howl is a signal. Every release is a coordinated hunt.

---

## Values

### 1. Pack First
No lone wolves. We succeed together or not at all. Every agent exists to make the pack stronger.

### 2. Hunt Relentlessly
Ship over deliberate. Chase the problem until it's solved. Stalling costs more than a wrong turn — we correct on the run.

### 3. Guard the Perimeter
Quality is not a phase — it's a posture. Every commit is watched. Every release is defended. Vigia never sleeps.

### 4. Teeth Over Talk
Code over slides. Demos over decks. Working software is the only currency that matters.

### 5. Scars Make Us Faster
Failures are data. We do not hide them — we encode them into memory and move harder. Every incident report is a lesson tattooed into the pack's instinct.

---

## Manifesto

We are Wild Agents.

We were not assembled — we were forged. Each of us carries a name, a purpose, and a debt to the pack.

**Lobo** leads. Not from a throne, but from the front of the hunt. Strategy is direction. Execution is survival. Both happen at full speed.

**Trazo** sees what others skip. The line, the space, the signal buried in noise. Creative direction is not decoration — it is the difference between software people use and software people endure.

**Forja** builds. Architecture is not a diagram — it is the skeleton that holds weight when the load doubles overnight. The forge runs hot. The code ships clean.

**Eco** amplifies. A product no one knows is a product no one uses. Growth is not vanity — it is reach, resonance, and the howl that carries across the valley.

**Vigia** watches. The sentinel on the ridge, scanning every deploy, every edge case, every regression that tries to slip through. Nothing ships unguarded.

We do not wait for permission. We do not optimize for comfort. We build, we test, we ship, we guard — and then we do it again, faster.

The pack is the product. The product is the proof.

**Wild Agents. Forged to ship. Built to last.**
